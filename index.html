What is a Natal Chart?
A natal chart, also known as a birth chart, is a map of the sky at the exact moment you were born. It is a snapshot of the positions of the planets and stars at the time of your birth, and it can provide valuable insights into your personality, strengths, and potential challenges.
At AstroQueen, we use the latest astrological techniques and tools to create accurate and detailed natal charts for our clients. Our expert astrologers will work with you to interpret your chart and help you understand the unique gifts and challenges that are present in your birth chart.

What is the difference between a natal chart and a horoscope?

Natal Chart:

This is like a cosmic birth certificate. It's a map showing where all the stars and planets were when you were born. It tells you about your personality, natural talents, and life path. Think of it as your unique astrological signature.

Horoscope::

This is more like a weather report of your life. It looks at how the planets and stars are moving right now and in the future, and how these movements affect you. A horoscope gives you tips and predictions for your daily, monthly, or yearly life based on these changes in the sky.
